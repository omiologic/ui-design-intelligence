# Marketing Page Brief

## Product

LedgerPilot is a B2B finance-operations product for founders and operators who
need clean cash-flow visibility before investor updates or board meetings.

## Audience

Seed-stage founders, fractional CFOs, and operations leads comparing tools that
can consolidate financial signals without replacing their accounting system.

## Primary Goal

Convince qualified visitors to request a product demo.

## Success Action

The visitor opens the demo request dialog and submits the form.

## Required Content

- Clear first-viewport promise around cash-flow clarity.
- Proof before feature depth: customer count, reporting time saved, and finance
  team trust.
- Feature explanation after proof, focused on runway view, anomaly alerts, and
  board-ready reporting.
- Objection handling for setup time, accounting-system compatibility, and data
  accuracy.
- Repeated but non-competing demo request CTA.

## Constraints

- Structural wireframe only; no final copy or visual mockup.
- One primary action per major region.
- Mobile must preserve the order: promise, demo CTA, proof, features, objection
  support, final action.
- Demo dialog must include closed, open, error, and success behavior.
