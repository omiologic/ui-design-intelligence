# Dashboard Brief

## Product

LedgerPilot operations dashboard for finance teams monitoring cash position,
forecast confidence, and reporting readiness.

## Audience

Fractional CFOs, founders, and finance operators who review company status
weekly and need to diagnose problems before investor or board reporting.

## Primary Goal

Help the operator understand current finance status, filter the view, inspect
diagnostics, and recover from stale or incomplete data.

## Success Action

The user applies a reporting-period filter, reviews the diagnostic table, and
opens the data freshness drawer when a warning appears.

## Required Content

- Status summary cards for cash balance, runway, forecast confidence, and data
  freshness.
- Controls for date range, entity, and scenario.
- Diagnostic table for accounts, anomaly status, and recommended action.
- Warning and recovery behavior for stale data.
- Responsive priority that keeps summary, warning, controls, and diagnostics
  usable on mobile.

## Constraints

- Structural dashboard wireframe only; no chart rendering or production data.
- Utility/product register, not marketing.
- Controls must include empty, loading, error, and success/recovery states.
- Prototype behavior must cover filter drawer, validation, stale-data detail,
  focus return, keyboard behavior, and responsive changes.
