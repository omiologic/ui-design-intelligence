# Product Page Brief

## Product

Northstar Beam 400, a rechargeable trail headlamp sold by an outdoor gear store.

## Audience

Trail runners, hikers, and campers comparing brightness, battery life, fit, and
shipping confidence before adding the item to cart.

## Primary Goal

Help shoppers inspect the product, compare key specs, choose a variant, and add
the item to cart with clear recovery when required choices are missing.

## Success Action

The shopper selects a color variant and adds the headlamp to cart.

## Required Content

- Product media and summary before supporting content.
- Price, rating proof, shipping promise, and stock status near the add-to-cart
  action.
- Variant selector with required-choice validation.
- Specification comparison before related products.
- Size/fit guide overlay for strap fit questions.
- Cart drawer and cart feedback after add-to-cart.

## Constraints

- Structural wireframe only; no final visual mockup.
- Product inspection and add-to-cart action outrank marketing content.
- Mobile order must preserve media, summary, variant selection, add-to-cart,
  specs, comparison, and related products.
- Cart drawer must include close, focus return, error/recovery, and success
  feedback behavior.
