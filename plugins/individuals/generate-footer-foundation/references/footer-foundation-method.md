# Footer Foundation Method

## Decision Heuristics

Prioritize recovery navigation, contact, legal, and industry-specific trust
needs.

## Anti-Pattern

Do not turn the footer into an unstructured list of every possible link.

## Worked Example

For a clinic, include address, phone, hours, map link, legal links, service
navigation, and appointment CTA as industry-specific needs.

## Hand-Off

Return JSON compatible with `footer-foundation.schema.json`.
